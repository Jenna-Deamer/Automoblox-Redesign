About Automoblox

Our History

Founded in 2004 by Patrick Calello, Automoblox has been a pioneer in reimagining the classic toy car. Inspired by a passion for both cars and design, Calello set out to create a toy that would combine sleek aesthetics with hands-on creativity. The first Automoblox wooden toy cars hit the market in 2005, instantly capturing the imagination of children and parents alike. With their distinctive design and interchangeable parts, Automoblox cars offered a unique building experience that encouraged creativity and innovation.

Over the years, Automoblox has continued to innovate and expand its product line, introducing new models, accessories, and playsets to cater to a growing fanbase. From sleek sports cars to rugged trucks, each Automoblox vehicle is meticulously crafted with attention to detail and quality, ensuring endless hours of playtime fun.

Our Values

At Automoblox, our values serve as the guiding principles that shape everything we do. We believe in fostering a culture of creativity, innovation, and responsibility, both within our company and in the products we create.

Quality Craftsmanship

We take pride in our commitment to quality craftsmanship, ensuring that every Automoblox toy is meticulously designed and built to withstand the test of time. From the selection of premium materials to the precision engineering of each component, we prioritize excellence in every detail.

Inspiring Creativity

We believe in the power of play to inspire creativity and imagination. That's why we design our toys to be more than just vehicles – they're tools for exploration and discovery, encouraging children to dream, design, and build their own unique creations.
